The Long Road to Competence

My journey in tech didn’t start with brilliance. It started with confusion. Broken environments. StackOverflow tabs. The humbling realization that I didn’t know nearly as much as I thought I did.

Over time, something changed. I stopped trying to be impressive and started trying to understand. That shift made all the difference.

Staying Curious

The best engineers I’ve met aren’t the loudest. They’re the most curious. They ask better questions. They iterate faster. They treat mistakes as data.
